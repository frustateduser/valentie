## How It Works

1. **YES Button**: Click to celebrate with confetti animation and a rejection message.
2. **NO Button**: Attempts to click it, but the button moves away from your cursor! When you finally manage to click it, you'll receive a humorous reason why the answer could be "no"
3. **Dynamic Styling**: The result message appears centered on screen with a glowing effect
